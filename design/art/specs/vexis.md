# Vexis the Archmage — visual spec

**REF-36**, 32x32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `trait_def` — define a `trait` that grants a capability to a type.

## Curriculum cue

A `trait` is a named capability that a type can be granted: `trait Spell { fn cast(&self); }`. Define the trait once, and any type that implements it gains that power. Vexis is the tower master who grants traits to the world below — the staff she raises ends in a single glowing orb, the capability made visible and handed down. When the player writes `trait Spell { ... }`, they are doing what Vexis embodies: naming a power that types can then receive.

## Visual brief

The tallest, most imposing figure in the cast, centered and standing. A full **Crypt** mantle of office with **Oxblood** primary and **Wineflesh** lit folds — the deepest, most formal robe yet, doubled at the shoulders with **Inkblood** seam shadow so it reads heavier than the Guildmaster's. An **Old gold** collar yoke loops the shoulders with an **Antique brass** chain base and a **Brass leaf** specular run.

In the right hand, raised, a tall staff: a **Bronze** shaft (outlined in **Coalblack**), an **Antique brass** collar, an **Old gold** finial cup, and at the very top a small glowing orb — a **Mage glow** core ringed by a **Royal arcane** halo. This is the one arcane accent on the entire figure, the trait being granted, and it sits at the top of the frame so the eye reads it as the source of power.

Head: short **Stone grey** hair and a trimmed **Stone grey** beard with **Aged paper** highlights. **Basalt** brow and eye line. Skin in **Pink quartz** / **Dusty rose**. An **Old gold** mantle belt at the waist, **Pine** hem under-mantle, **Bronze**/**Bog umber** boots.

## Palette compliance

Burgundy (mantle): `K Y O W`. Skin: `P R`. Neutrals (hair, beard, outline): `X B S V`. Gold (collar yoke, belt, staff collar, finial): `N Z G L`. Magic (orb only): `* %` — two pixels of core and a thin halo, well under the 5% cap. Bog umber `U` (boot shadow), Pine `F` (hem).

No alarm scarlet, no specular white, no cobalt, no teal, no forest beyond the pine hem.

## Lighting

Top-left 45°. The mantle's left-facing folds catch **Wineflesh**; the right side falls to **Crypt** and **Inkblood**. The brightest mass is the **Mage glow** orb at the staff top — the granted capability — with the **Brass leaf** specular on the collar yoke as the secondary read. Authority signaled in gold; power signaled in violet, top and center.
