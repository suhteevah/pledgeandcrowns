# The Cooper — visual spec

**REF-15**, 32×32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `vec_iter` — teaches `Vec<T>` and `.iter().sum()`.

## Curriculum cue

A `Vec<T>` is a barrel of items. The Cooper is the village barrel-maker — he physically constructs the data structure the lesson is named after. His tutorial frames the iterator triple as "open the lid, walk every item, do something with each, close the lid." The sprite reinforces the metaphor by placing him next to a small wooden barrel — visibly his work, visibly a container, visibly enumerable.

## Visual brief

Burly human male, mid-height, broad-chested. Wears a **Bronze**/**Bog umber** leather work-vest over a **Hayfield**-cream undershirt (a touch of light against the dark vest), brown trousers in **Bronze**/**Bog umber**. Leather work-gloves in **Bog umber**. Skin uses the standard Pink quartz / Dusty rose / Wineflesh ramp. Hair short, **Crypt**/**Basalt** — he's the dark-haired counterpoint to the gold-haired Smith and Bellringer.

Beside him, occupying the right third of the canvas, stands a small barrel: a vertical stack of three wooden staves (**Bronze** core with **Bog umber** shadows and **Antique brass** highlight on the lit side) bound by two **Stone grey** iron hoops with a single **Brass leaf** highlight pixel each. The lid of the barrel is **Old gold** (a hint at the contents being valuable — coins, grain, ale). The barrel sits at his right hand, which rests on top of it, asserting ownership.

In his left hand he holds a small **Basalt**-handled adze (a curved cooper's tool) at hip height, **Stone grey** blade. Belt is **Bronze** with **Old gold** buckle.

## Palette compliance

Burgundy (skin): `R W P`. Gold (barrel hoops highlight, vest stripe, lid): `N G L`. Neutrals (hair, outline, hoops, blade): `X Y B S`. Forest (none — Cooper is keyed entirely to the warm/wood family). Other gold (vest, trousers, gloves, barrel staves): `U Z N`. Hayfield (undershirt): `Q`.

No magic violet, no scarlet, no specular white, no Cobalt, no teal.

## Lighting

Top-left 45°. Barrel staves' left edge gets the Antique-brass highlight; right edge stays in Bog umber. Vest shoulder gets a tiny Antique brass highlight on its left side.
