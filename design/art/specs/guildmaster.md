# The Guildmaster — visual spec

**REF-30**, 32x32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `impl_method` — give a struct behavior with an `impl` block.

## Curriculum cue

A bare struct holds data; an `impl` block gives it duties. The Guildmaster is the canonical "member with responsibilities" — a person who *is* a guild member (the struct) and who *does* the work of office (the methods). The medallion of office on his chest is the type's identity; the staff of office is the method he wields. When the player writes `impl Member { fn rank(&self) -> ... }`, they are doing what the Guildmaster embodies: binding behavior to the named type.

## Visual brief

A tall, dignified authority figure, centered and standing. Long **Crypt** robe of office with **Oxblood** primary and **Wineflesh** lit folds — the house burgundy uniform, but the most formal cut in the cast: full-length, deep, unbroken by tradesman's apron or tunic. **Inkblood** is available for the deepest shadow seams.

On the chest, a guild medallion: an **Old gold** disc on an **Antique brass** chain that loops over the shoulders, with a single **Brass leaf** specular highlight on the disc's upper-left curve. This is the seal of the type — the one bright gold mass on the figure's body.

In his right hand, planted on the ground, a staff of office: an **Antique brass** collar, a **Bronze** shaft (outlined in **Coalblack**), and an **Old gold** finial cap at the top with a **Brass leaf** specular pixel — the staff he wields, the method made visible.

Head: short **Stone grey** hair and a trimmed **Stone grey** beard with **Aged paper** highlights — older, senior. **Basalt** brow and eye line. Skin in **Pink quartz** / **Dusty rose**. A standard **Old gold** belt buckle at the waist, **Pine** hem under-robe, **Bronze**/**Bog umber** boots.

## Palette compliance

Burgundy (robe): `K Y O W`. Skin: `P R`. Neutrals (hair, beard, outline): `X B S V`. Gold (medallion, chain, staff, buckle, finial): `N Z G L`. Bog umber `U` (boot shadow), Pine `F` (hem).

No magic violet, no alarm scarlet, no specular white, no cobalt, no teal, no forest beyond the pine hem.

## Lighting

Top-left 45°. The robe's left-facing folds catch **Wineflesh**; the right side falls to **Crypt**. The two brightest accents are the **Brass leaf** spec on the medallion disc and the **Brass leaf** spec on the staff finial — authority signaled in gold, top and center.
