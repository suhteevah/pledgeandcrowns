# The Smith — visual spec

**REF-11**, 32×32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `mut_binding` — teaches `let mut` and the compound-assignment operator.

## Curriculum cue

The Smith is the village blacksmith. He turns iron into something new by repeated application of force — that's *mutation*. His pose, palette, and props all reinforce "things change here, on purpose, with the right opt-in." The single piece of advice he repeats in his tutorial is "name what changes." His sprite must read that way at a glance.

## Visual brief

Stocky human male, broad shoulders, leather apron, thick forearms. Stands square-on (front-facing) gripping a hammer at his right side. The hammer's head is iron with a Brass-leaf rim (the strike point is the only specular bright on the whole sprite — it's where mutation *happens*). The apron is **Oxblood** with **Wineflesh** mid-tone and **Crypt** shadow — burgundy is the brand, the Smith wears it as the village's craft-uniform. Beneath the apron, a **Pine**-shadowed undershirt; brown leather belt with **Old gold** buckle. Boots are **Bronze**/**Bog umber**.

Skin is the same pale palette as REF-01 (Pink quartz / Dusty rose / Wineflesh), to anchor him as part of the same human cast. Beard is **Bronze**/**Antique brass** — the gold ramp does double-duty as warm hair. Eyes a single dark pixel each.

The hammer head is **Basalt** (iron core) with **Stone grey** mid-tone and a single **Brass leaf** highlight pixel on the strike face. Haft is **Bronze** with **Bog umber** shadow. He grips it diagonally so the head sits at his right hip, signalling "tool ready, not in use" — same idiom as the player character at rest.

## Palette compliance

Burgundy ramp (apron): `K Y O W R P`. Gold ramp (buckle, hammer rim, beard): `Z N G L`. Forest (undershirt shadow): `F`. Neutrals (hammer iron, outline): `X B S`. Skin: `P R W`. Boots: `Z U`.

No magic violet, no alarm scarlet (the forge fire belongs to the Iron Vale Forge zone in Act 6, not on this sprite), no specular white.

## Lighting

Top-left 45°. Highlights on the left edges of apron, beard, and hammer head. Shadows down-right.
