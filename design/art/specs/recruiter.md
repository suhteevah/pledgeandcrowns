# The Recruiter — visual spec

**REF-31**, 32x32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `assoc_new` — `Self::new(name)` returns a fresh instance.

## Curriculum cue

`Self::new` is the forge for a brand-new member: hand it a name, get back a fully-formed instance. The Recruiter is the constructor personified. He holds the open recruit-roll — the parchment where a name becomes a member — and a quill mid-stamp. The act of stamping the roll *is* `Self::new`: a name in, a new record out. `Self` is the guild; `new` is the entry he writes.

## Visual brief

A standing officer, slightly turned, working. **Crypt** robe with **Oxblood** primary and **Wineflesh** lit folds, **Antique brass** collar studs at the throat — clerical-formal, plainer than the Guildmaster.

In the left hand, held open across the body, the recruit-roll: a **Parchment cream** page on a **Bronze** spindle rod, with three short **Coalblack** text lines (the existing entries) and a fresh **Old gold** wax seal pressed near the bottom — the new member, just forged. An **Aged paper** shadow row gives the page its curl.

In the right hand, raised, a **Coalblack** quill with a **Brass leaf** specular pixel on the nib — caught mid-stamp, the single brightest point. The hand reads in **Pink quartz** / **Dusty rose**.

Head: short **Stone grey** hair with **Aged paper** highlight, clean-shaven, **Basalt** brow and eyes, skin in **Pink quartz** / **Dusty rose**. Standard **Old gold** belt buckle, **Pine** hem, **Bronze**/**Bog umber** boots.

## Palette compliance

Burgundy (robe): `Y O W`. Skin: `P R`. Neutrals (hair, outline, quill, roll text): `X B S V`. Gold (collar stud, spindle, wax seal, quill nib spec): `N Z G L`. Parchment cream `C` (roll page). Bog umber `U` (boots), Pine `F` (hem).

No magic violet, no alarm scarlet, no specular white, no cobalt, no teal, no forest beyond the pine hem.

## Lighting

Top-left 45° onto the open roll. The roll's upper edge catches **Parchment cream**; the lower curl shades toward **Aged paper**. The single brightest pixel is the **Brass leaf** quill nib — the new member is being written *now*.
