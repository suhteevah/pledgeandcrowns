# The Drillmaster — visual spec

**REF-24**, 32×32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `for_in_range` — `for i in 0..10`.

## Curriculum cue

The Drillmaster teaches the bounded counted loop. The half-open range `0..10` is a *cadence* — count out the steps, stop one before the upper bound, never overshoot. The visual idiom is a parade-ground captain: rigid posture, baton tucked under the right arm, left hand counting cadence on the fingers. The iconography: a small parade-ground **chalk slate** strapped to the waist with chalked tally marks on it — the iteration counter made literal.

Voice spec (no editor note exists yet for this character; the Drillmaster's tone is implicit in the mission's "walk a known sequence" framing): clipped, declarative, parade-ground.

## Visual brief

Tall, square-shouldered human in a uniform tunic — **Forest** mid, **Pine** shadow, **Spring meadow** highlight on the left shoulder — over **Bronze** trousers tucked into **Bog umber** boots. **Antique brass** epaulettes (one pixel each shoulder) and a **Old gold** chest-stripe across the front of the tunic from left shoulder to right hip — military, not heraldic. **Stone grey** hair shorn close, square jaw, a single **Bronze** moustache row.

Right arm held tight against the side, a short **Crypt** baton tucked under the upper arm with a **Brass leaf** end-cap pip just visible at the right hip. Left hand held forward at chest height, palm out, **three fingers** raised distinctly (this is the visible cadence count — the iteration in progress). Skin standard human ramp.

A small slate at the right hip, strapped with **Bronze**: 4×3 patch of **Coalblack** with three **Aged paper** tally pixels chalked across it — `| | |` cadence. The slate's **Stone grey** frame keeps it readable against the dark tunic.

Stance: feet shoulder-width, perfectly square, weight even. The Smith was square-on; the Drillmaster is square-on AND symmetrical.

## Palette compliance

Forest ramp (tunic): `J F E H`. Gold ramp (chest stripe, epaulettes, slate strap, baton cap, moustache): `Z N G L`. Burgundy (baton): `K Y`. Neutrals (hair, outline, slate, chalk): `X B S V`. Skin: `P R W`. Boots: `U`.

No magic violet, no alarm scarlet, no specular white, no cobalt, no teal.

## Lighting

Top-left 45°. The chalked tallies on the slate are the second-brightest element after the chest-stripe — readable as the iteration counter. Body shadow on the right side keeps the silhouette crisp against the village ground.
