# The Cartographer — visual spec

**REF-12**, 32×32, idle, transparent bg.
**Mission:** `if_else_sign` — teaches `if`/`else if`/`else` as branching expressions.

## Curriculum cue

The Cartographer maps roads. Roads are how decisions look from above: at every fork, you take *one* path and not the others. Her tutorial frames `if/else` as "branch on a question, every road goes somewhere, every branch produces a value." The sprite leans into that: she carries a *rolled map* on one arm (the question) and gestures with the other (the branch). She is the visual mnemonic for control flow.

## Visual brief

Slim human female, traveller's build. Stands three-quarter front. Wears a **Cobalt-deep** travelling cloak with **Cobalt** mid-tone — she's the only Act-1 prelude NPC keyed off the cool counterweight, because mapping the world is the perspective-from-outside it. Hood pulled back, long hair in **Crypt** / **Basalt**, with a single **Stone grey** highlight strand on the lit side.

Across her chest, a **Bronze** strap with **Old gold** buckle holds the rolled **Aged paper** map at her left hip — visible as a compact roll with **Antique brass** end-caps and the corner of an unrolled **Parchment cream** flap showing one **Forest**-green road glyph and one **Old gold** crossroads marker (this is the only place on the sprite where the player can see a *literal* branch — the road forks into two on the map's exposed corner). Skin is the standard Pink quartz / Dusty rose / Wineflesh pale ramp. Belt is **Bronze**, boots **Bog umber**.

She holds a small brass **compass** in her right hand at waist height — `Old gold` ring, `Coalblack` needle, `Pink quartz` north tick. The compass is what tells you which branch you're on.

## Palette compliance

Burgundy (skin only): `R W P`. Gold (compass, buckles, map ends, road marker): `Z N G L`. Cool (cloak): `< >`. Neutrals (outline, hair, map paper): `X Y B S V C`. Forest (road glyph): `E`. Boots: `U`.

No magic violet, no specular white, no scarlet.

## Lighting

Top-left 45°. Cloak left edge gets the Cobalt mid-tone highlight; right side stays in Cobalt deep.
