## Suggestions for the cast as a whole

- **Wave-2 self-reference tic** (`"the X verbs:"` inside the quoted line) appears on Auditor, Chronicler, Alchemist, Heraldic Sage. It dilutes terseness. House style going forward: the metaphor stands alone in the quote; the narration sentence outside the quote can name the NPC's action (`the Chronicler dips his quill`), but the quoted line should not say `the Chronicler` again. Removing this on this pass.
- **Verb diversity per NPC** — most pass flavors lead with one craft verb (`taps`, `pulls`, `claps`, `hoops`, `decants`...). This is the right pattern. Future NPCs should follow it: one verb that only they use.
- **Ledger / scroll / quill** — three writing implements split across Reckoner (ledger+ink), Chronicler (quill), Herald (scroll), Auditor (ledger, post-fix → tally/books). After this pass: Reckoner=ledger, Chronicler=quill, Herald=scroll, Auditor=tally. Clean.
